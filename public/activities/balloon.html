Hot Air Balloon Rides

Half-day & full-day adventures

Private & group experiences

BOOK YOUR RIDE

Float above Morocco’s stunning landscapes and watch the sunrise over the desert on our Hot Air Balloon adventures. Drift peacefully above golden dunes, vibrant oases, and the Atlas Mountains for unforgettable views. Each ride is guided by experienced pilots ensuring both safety and an extraordinary experience.

This is more than a ride — it’s a unique Morocco adventure that reveals the country’s beauty from above. Witness traditional villages, endless sandscapes, and the occasional desert wildlife, all from the comfort of your balloon basket.

After landing, enjoy a traditional Moroccan mint tea and light refreshments while sharing stories with your group. Our trips are intimate, keeping the experience personal and memorable for every guest.

Whether you’re seeking breathtaking photography, a peaceful morning, or a bucket-list adventure, our hot air balloon rides over Morocco promise memories that will last a lifetime.

DURATION

Approximately 3 hours (half-day)

MEETING POINT

Pick-up from your Marrakech riad or main city hotels at 5:00 AM

WHAT'S INCLUDED

  • Hot air balloon flight over the Moroccan desert
  • Experienced pilot and ground crew
  • Traditional Moroccan mint tea and refreshments
  • Private and group tour options

NOTES

Wear comfortable clothing and closed shoes. The experience is designed for small groups to maintain an intimate and safe adventure.

Book Your Hot Air Balloon Experience

We’ll confirm your reservation within 24 hours. For questions, email us at halo@halimmoroccojourneys.com or message us on Instagram @halimmoroccojourneys.
