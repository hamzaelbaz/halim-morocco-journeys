Surfing Experience

Half-day & full-day surf tours

Private lessons & group sessions

RESERVE YOUR SPOT

Feel the thrill of riding Morocco’s Atlantic waves along world-famous beaches like Agadir, Essaouira, and Taghazout. Our surfing experience is perfect for all skill levels, offering expert guidance in a safe and exciting environment.

This isn’t just a lesson — it’s a full immersion into Morocco’s surf culture. You’ll learn the rhythms of the ocean, practice your technique on soft waves, and enjoy the unique vibe of coastal Morocco, all under the guidance of professional instructors.

After the session, relax on the sandy beach, sip a refreshing drink, and watch the sunset over the Atlantic. Groups are small to ensure personalized attention and maximum fun — whether you’re a beginner or an experienced surfer.

Whether you’re here for the waves, the ocean breeze, or the vibrant surf culture, this is your chance to ride Morocco’s most iconic beaches.

DURATION

Half-day & full-day tours

MEETING POINT

Main beach entrance, Agadir, 9:00 AM sharp.

WHAT'S INCLUDED

  • Professional surfing instructors
  • Surfboard and safety gear provided
  • Beach guidance and local tips
  • Refreshments after your session

NOTES

Bring swimwear, a towel, and sunscreen. Groups are kept small to ensure personalized attention and safety.
