Sahara Desert Trekking

Half-day & full-day desert tours

Private and group trekking options

RESERVE YOUR SPOT

Embark on an unforgettable Sahara trekking adventure across Morocco’s iconic golden dunes. Follow ancient desert paths, witness breathtaking landscapes, and immerse yourself in the peaceful vastness of the Sahara. Guided by experienced local explorers, every step tells a story of the desert’s timeless beauty.

This trekking experience is designed for all levels — from first-time hikers to seasoned adventurers. Feel the soft sand under your feet, discover hidden oases, and capture the perfect panoramic views that only the Sahara can offer.

Along the journey, enjoy short breaks to appreciate the serenity, take photos, and learn about the local Berber culture from your guide. Our small group sizes ensure a personal and safe adventure in this majestic desert.

Whether you seek adventure, photography, or a peaceful escape, Sahara trekking offers an experience that will stay with you long after the journey ends.

DURATION

Half-day & full-day trekking tours available

MEETING POINT

Central Sahara camp, ready to start at 8:00 AM or 2:00 PM

WHAT'S INCLUDED

  • Guided trekking across the Sahara Desert
  • Experienced local guide sharing cultural insights
  • Water and light snacks during the trek
  • Photo opportunities and sunset stops

NOTES

Wear comfortable trekking shoes and sun protection. Suitable for all fitness levels. Small groups ensure safety and a personalized experience.
