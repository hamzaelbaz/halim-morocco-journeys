FAQS

Common questions
What types of tours and activities do you offer? +

We offer a wide range of experiences across Morocco, including city tours in Marrakech, Casablanca, Essaouira, Fes, Agadir, Ouarzazate, and Chefchaouen, as well as desert trekking, camel rides, quad biking, surfing, and hot air balloon adventures.

Are your tours suitable for families? +

Yes! All our activities and tours are designed for travelers of all ages, including children, ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone.

Do you provide guides and equipment? +

All tours include professional local guides. For activities like surfing, quad biking, or camel rides, we provide all necessary equipment and safety gear.

Do you offer transportation? +

Yes, we provide pick-up and drop-off from hotels or convenient locations for all tours and activities across Morocco.

What should I bring on my tour or activity? +

Comfortable shoes, sunscreen, water, and a camera are recommended. Specific activities may have additional requirements, which we will share before your tour.

What is your cancellation policy? +

You can cancel up to 48 hours in advance for a full refund. Last-minute cancellations may receive partial refunds depending on the tour or activity.
