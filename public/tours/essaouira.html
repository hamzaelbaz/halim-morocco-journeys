Essaouira City

Half-day & full-day tours

Private & group options

RESERVE YOUR SPOT

Escape to Morocco’s coast with our Essaouira day tour. Known as the “Wind City of Africa,” Essaouira blends history, culture, and ocean views in one unforgettable experience. Stroll along the UNESCO-listed medina, wander through vibrant souks, and breathe in the fresh Atlantic air.

With a local guide, you’ll explore the historic fishing port, watch colorful boats come and go, and enjoy the city’s relaxed seaside charm. Essaouira is also famous for its art galleries, traditional thuya wood workshops, and delicious fresh seafood.

Whether you’re here for history, culture, or simply to relax by the sea, Essaouira offers the perfect balance of adventure and tranquility.

DURATION

Full-day tour (approx. 10 hours round trip)

MEETING POINT

Pick-up from your hotel or riad in Marrakech

WHAT'S INCLUDED

  • Round-trip transfer from Marrakech
  • Guided walking tour of Essaouira’s medina
  • Visit to the fishing port and city ramparts
  • Free time to explore shops and enjoy fresh seafood

NOTES

Bring comfortable walking shoes, sunglasses, and a light jacket. Essaouira is often breezy. Private and group tours available.
