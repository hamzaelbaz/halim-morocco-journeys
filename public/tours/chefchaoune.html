Chefchaouen City

Day trips & multi-day tours

Private & group options

BOOK YOUR TOUR

Step into the heart of Morocco’s “Blue Pearl” with our guided Chefchaouen tour. Wander through narrow streets painted in endless shades of blue, where every corner feels like a work of art. From hidden courtyards to vibrant souks, Chefchaouen invites you to slow down and discover its magic.

Led by a local guide, you’ll explore the medina, learn about Andalusian-Moroccan culture, and visit artisan workshops where traditional crafts are still alive. The calm atmosphere of Chefchaouen is unlike any other Moroccan city, making it the perfect place to relax and connect with the surroundings.

Our tours are designed for small groups or private travelers, giving you time to enjoy the mountain views, taste authentic dishes, and capture unforgettable photos of Morocco’s most picturesque city.

Whether you come for the colors, the culture, or the peaceful charm of the Rif mountains, Chefchaouen is an experience that will stay with you.

DURATION

Half-day or full-day tour

MEETING POINT

Central Chefchaouen – details provided after booking

WHAT'S INCLUDED

  • Guided tour of Chefchaouen’s blue medina
  • Visit to local artisan shops and markets
  • Cultural insights from a local expert
  • Free time to explore and take photos

NOTES

Wear comfortable walking shoes and bring your camera. Private and group tours available on request.
